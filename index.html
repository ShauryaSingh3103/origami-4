What is origami?

About Me Follow Me

Origami (折り紙, Japanese pronunciation: [oɾiɡami] or [oɾiꜜɡami],
from ori meaning "folding", and kami meaning
"paper" (kami changes to gami due to rendaku)) is the art of paper folding,
which is often associated with Japanese culture.
